Himachal Tour Itinerary Options

2 Nights / 3 Days – Shimla Mountain Escape

Day 1 – Arrival in Shimla
Arrival at Shimla. Transfer to hotel and check-in. Evening free to explore Mall Road, The Ridge and Christ Church. Enjoy cool mountain weather and local shopping. Overnight stay in Shimla.

Day 2 – Kufri & Local Sightseeing
After breakfast proceed for Kufri excursion. Visit Himalayan Nature Park and enjoy panoramic Himalayan views. Later visit Jakhoo Temple and explore local markets. Overnight stay in Shimla.

Day 3 – Departure
Breakfast at hotel. Check-out and transfer for onward journey with beautiful mountain memories.

Ideal for short romantic getaway and weekend family trips.

3 Nights / 4 Days – Manali Scenic Holiday

Day 1 – Arrival & Leisure
Arrival in Manali. Hotel check-in. Evening at leisure to explore Mall Road and local cafes. Overnight stay in Manali.

Day 2 – Manali Local Sightseeing
Visit Hadimba Devi Temple, Vashisht Hot Springs, Tibetan Monastery and Club House. Enjoy evening shopping at Mall Road. Overnight stay.

Day 3 – Solang Valley / Rohtang Pass
Excursion to Solang Valley for snow activities (seasonal). Visit Rohtang Pass subject to permit and weather conditions. Return to hotel. Overnight stay.

Day 4 – Departure
Breakfast and check-out. Transfer for onward journey.

Perfect for honeymoon couples and adventure lovers.

5 Nights / 6 Days – Complete Shimla & Manali Circuit

Day 1 – Arrival in Shimla
Transfer to hotel. Evening Mall Road visit and leisure time.

Day 2 – Kufri & Shimla Local
Excursion to Kufri. Visit Jakhoo Temple and explore Ridge area.

Day 3 – Transfer to Manali
Scenic drive from Shimla to Manali (approx 7–8 hrs). Enroute sightseeing. Hotel check-in and rest.

Day 4 – Manali Local Sightseeing
Hadimba Temple, Vashisht, Club House and Mall Road.

Day 5 – Solang Valley / Rohtang Pass
Adventure activities and snow experience (seasonal).

Day 6 – Departure
Breakfast and transfer for onward journey.

Most popular Himachal family and honeymoon package.

Goa Holiday Itinerary Options

3 Nights / 4 Days – Classic Goa Holiday

Day 1 – Arrival in Goa
Arrival at Goa Airport / Railway Station. Transfer to hotel and check-in. Evening free to relax at beach or enjoy sunset. Overnight stay in Goa.

Day 2 – North Goa Sightseeing
Visit Calangute Beach, Baga Beach, Candolim Beach and Fort Aguada. Optional water sports activities. Evening leisure at beach shacks. Overnight stay.

Day 3 – South Goa Sightseeing
Visit Miramar Beach, Dona Paula, Basilica of Bom Jesus and Old Goa Churches. Mandovi River cruise (optional). Overnight stay.

Day 4 – Departure
Breakfast and check-out. Transfer for onward journey.

Perfect for couples, friends and short beach getaway.

Free day for water sports, shopping or private beach time.

Breakfast and check-out.

Ideal for honeymoon couples and family holidays.

Kerala Tour Itinerary Options

3 Nights / 4 Days – Munnar & Alleppey Escape

Day 1 – Arrival in Kochi → Munnar Transfer
Arrival at Kochi Airport/Railway Station. Scenic drive to Munnar. Check-in at hotel. Evening leisure amidst tea gardens. Overnight stay in Munnar.

Day 2 – Munnar Sightseeing
Visit Tea Museum, Mattupetty Dam, Echo Point and Eravikulam National Park. Enjoy panoramic hill views. Overnight stay.

Day 3 – Munnar → Alleppey
Transfer to Alleppey. Experience backwaters (houseboat stay optional). Relaxing evening by the lake. Overnight stay.

Day 4 – Departure
Breakfast and transfer to Kochi for onward journey.

Perfect blend of hill station and backwater experience.

4 Nights / 5 Days – Kerala Classic Tour

Day 1 – Kochi Arrival
Arrival and local sightseeing including Fort Kochi, Chinese Fishing Nets and Marine Drive.

Day 2 – Kochi → Munnar
Transfer to Munnar. Evening at leisure in tea estates.

Day 3 – Munnar Sightseeing
Mattupetty Dam, Echo Point, Eravikulam Park.

Day 4 – Alleppey Houseboat
Transfer to Alleppey for houseboat stay with scenic backwaters experience.

Day 5 – Departure
Transfer to Kochi Airport/Railway Station.

Ideal for honeymoon couples and relaxed family vacations.

North East India Tour Itinerary Options

4 Nights / 5 Days – Sikkim Highlights

Day 1 – Arrival in Gangtok
Arrival at Bagdogra Airport / NJP Railway Station. Transfer to Gangtok. Check-in and evening leisure. Overnight stay.

Day 2 – Tsomgo Lake & Baba Mandir
Excursion to Tsomgo Lake and Baba Harbhajan Singh Temple (subject to weather/permit). Return to hotel.

Day 3 – Gangtok Local Sightseeing
Visit Rumtek Monastery, Do Drul Chorten, Enchey Monastery and local markets.

Day 4 – Transfer to Darjeeling
Scenic drive to Darjeeling. Evening leisure.

Day 5 – Departure
Morning Tiger Hill (optional) and transfer for onward journey.

Perfect for nature lovers and scenic mountain experience.

5 Nights / 6 Days – Meghalaya Explorer

Day 1 – Arrival in Guwahati → Shillong
Transfer to Shillong. Visit Umiam Lake enroute.

Day 2 – Cherrapunjee Excursion
Visit Seven Sisters Falls, Nohkalikai Falls and Mawsmai Cave.

Day 3 – Living Root Bridge
Explore Nongriat Living Root Bridge and scenic viewpoints.

Day 4 – Dawki & Mawlynnong
Crystal clear Umngot River boating and Asia’s cleanest village.

Day 5 – Shillong Local
Ward’s Lake, Cathedral Church and local markets.

Day 6 – Departure
Transfer back to Guwahati Airport.

Ideal for adventure seekers and offbeat travel lovers.
