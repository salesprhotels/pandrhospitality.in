Domestic Tour Itineraries

Kashmir Valley Tour – 5 Days

Day 1 – Arrival Srinagar

Arrival at Srinagar airport and transfer to hotel or houseboat. Enjoy evening Shikara ride on Dal Lake and explore floating market.

Day 2 – Gulmarg Excursion

Drive to Gulmarg known for gondola cable car ride and snow activities. Enjoy breathtaking views of snow mountains.

Day 3 – Pahalgam Valley

Drive to Pahalgam and explore Betaab Valley, Aru Valley and Lidder river surroundings.

Day 4 – Srinagar Sightseeing

Visit famous Mughal Gardens including Nishat Bagh, Shalimar Bagh and Chashme Shahi. Evening shopping at Lal Chowk market.

Day 5 – Departure Srinagar

After breakfast transfer to Srinagar airport for onward journey.

Patnitop Hill Retreat – 3 Days

Day 1 – Arrival Jammu & Transfer to Patnitop

Arrival at Jammu airport or railway station. Drive to Patnitop hill station located in the Shivalik range. Check in to hotel and enjoy evening walk in pine forests.

Day 2 – Patnitop Sightseeing

Visit Nag Temple, Sanasar meadow and enjoy adventure activities like zipline and paragliding. Spend the day enjoying mountain views and cool climate.

Day 3 – Departure Jammu

After breakfast drive back to Jammu airport or railway station for return journey.

Shimla Hill Tour – 3 Days

Day 1 – Arrival Shimla

Arrival at Shimla from Delhi or Chandigarh. Check-in to hotel and relax. Evening visit to Mall Road, The Ridge and Christ Church for a pleasant walk.

Day 2 – Kufri Excursion

After breakfast drive to Kufri for sightseeing. Enjoy horse riding, Himalayan Nature Park and scenic views. Return to Shimla and visit Jakhu Temple and Lakkar Bazaar.

Day 3 – Departure Shimla

After breakfast check-out from hotel and drive back to Delhi or Chandigarh with beautiful memories.

Manali Adventure Tour – 4 Days

Day 1 – Arrival Manali

Arrival at Manali bus stand or from Delhi overnight journey. Check-in to hotel and relax. Evening explore Mall Road and Tibetan Monastery.

Day 2 – Solang Valley Excursion

Visit Solang Valley for adventure activities like paragliding, skiing and ropeway. Optional visit to Atal Tunnel and Sissu village.

Day 3 – Manali Local Sightseeing

Visit Hadimba Temple, Manu Temple, Vashisht hot springs and Van Vihar. Evening explore Old Manali cafes.

Day 4 – Departure Manali

Check-out after breakfast and begin return journey to Delhi or Chandigarh.

Sikkim Gangtok Lachung Lachen Tour – 6 Days

Day 1 – Arrival Gangtok

Arrival at Bagdogra airport or NJP railway station. Drive to Gangtok through scenic Teesta river valley. Check-in to hotel and evening free to explore MG Marg market.

Day 2 – Gangtok Local Sightseeing

Visit Tsomgo Lake, Baba Mandir and enjoy panoramic Himalayan views. Later explore local monasteries and viewpoints in Gangtok.

Day 3 – Gangtok to Lachen

Drive to Lachen through beautiful mountain villages. Visit Singhik viewpoint and enjoy views of Mount Kanchenjunga.

Day 4 – Gurudongmar Lake and Lachung

Early morning visit to Gurudongmar Lake located at high altitude. Later drive to Lachung village for overnight stay.

Day 5 – Yumthang Valley Excursion

Visit Yumthang Valley also called Valley of Flowers and enjoy natural hot springs. Later drive back to Gangtok.

Day 6 – Departure

After breakfast transfer to Bagdogra airport or NJP railway station for departure.

Darjeeling Gangtok Tour – 6 Days

Day 1 – Arrival Bagdogra and Transfer to Darjeeling

Arrival at Bagdogra airport or NJP railway station. Drive through tea gardens and mountain roads to Darjeeling. Check-in to hotel and evening free to explore Mall Road.

Day 2 – Darjeeling Sightseeing

Early morning visit to Tiger Hill to witness sunrise over Mount Kanchenjunga. Later visit Ghoom Monastery, Batasia Loop, Himalayan Mountaineering Institute and Tea Gardens.

Day 3 – Darjeeling to Gangtok

Drive from Darjeeling to Gangtok through scenic Himalayan roads. Check-in to hotel and evening explore MG Marg market.

Day 4 – Tsomgo Lake and Baba Mandir

Visit the famous Tsomgo Lake and Baba Harbhajan Singh Temple located near the Indo-China border.

Day 5 – Gangtok Local Sightseeing

Visit Rumtek Monastery, Tashi Viewpoint, Ganesh Tok and Hanuman Tok. Enjoy local shopping in Gangtok.

Day 6 – Return to Bagdogra

After breakfast drive back to Bagdogra airport or NJP railway station for departure.
